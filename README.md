**Startup Performance Intelligence Dashboard
Project Overview**

This project analyzes startup performance across multiple industries using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into startup outcomes, revenue generation, burn rates, investor activity, market size, and founder backgrounds.

**Objectives**
Analyze startup success and failure patterns.
Compare revenue across startup outcomes.
Identify sectors with high burn rates.
Evaluate investor participation trends.
Discover market opportunities across industries.

**Tools Used**
Power BI
Power Query
DAX
Excel
Data Visualization

**Dashboard Features**
Interactive slicers for sector and outcome filtering.
KPI cards for revenue and burn rate analysis.
Revenue trend analysis by startup outcome.
Investor type distribution analysis.
Market size comparison across sectors.
Dedicated insights page with business recommendations.

**Key Insights**
IPO startups generated the highest average revenue.
Fintech recorded the highest burn rate.
Tier-2 VC investors showed the highest investment activity.
Crypto, Fintech, and Health sectors represented the largest market sizes.
Successful startups consistently outperformed failed startups in revenue generation.
